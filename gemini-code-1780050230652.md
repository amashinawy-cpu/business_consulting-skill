# Strategic Business Analysis & Restructuring Workflow

## Phase 1: Discovery & Baseline Assessment (Current State)
- **Stakeholder Vision Alignment:** Map executive mandates, shareholder expectations, resource constraints, and overall organizational vision.
- **Operational & Structural Auditing:** Review existing organizational charts, functional duplications, communication silos, and resource misallocations across subsidiaries or business units.
- **Technology & Infrastructure Baseline:** Evaluate the digital maturity curve, legacy system dependencies, and readiness for automated or centralized operations.

## Phase 2: Diagnostic & Synergy Matrix (Analysis State)
- **Operational Gap & Friction Analysis:** Core gaps identified during Phase 1 are mapped against macro-market opportunities and regulatory updates.
- **Technical & Sustainable Feasibility Modeling:** Evaluate the feasibility of integrating smart infrastructure, centralized shared-services models (e.g., centralizing HR, Finance, and IT under a holding company), or clean-tech solutions.
- **Absorptive Capacity Assessment:** Quantify the organization's human capital readiness to absorb change, identifying clear training vectors and change-management champions.

## Phase 3: Target Operating Model & Executive Roadmap (Future State)
- **Target Operating Model (TOM) Architecture:** Design optimized corporate structures, governance matrices, and clear reporting hierarchies (e.g., Subsidiary Boards reporting to a Holding Company Board).
- **Phased Implementation Horizons:** 
  - *Horizon 1 (Immediate / 0–90 Days):* Low-hanging fruit, quick wins, and critical restructuring setups.
  - *Horizon 2 (Mid-Term / 3–6 Months):* Centralization of shared services, process optimization, and technology rollouts.
  - *Horizon 3 (Long-Term / 6–12+ Months):* Full operational stabilization, continuous audit loops, and ROI realization.
- **Strategic Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Define clear, quantifiable operational and financial metrics to monitor the success of the intervention.
